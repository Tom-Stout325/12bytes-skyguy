Transaction Form Help

Overview

Use this form to enter any financial transaction related to your business.

Field Descriptions

  • Date: The date the transaction occurred.
  • Type: Choose either “Income” or “Expense”.
  • Sub-Category: Select the appropriate classification for tax and reporting purposes.
  • Amount: Enter the total amount of the transaction. Use negative numbers for expenses if needed.
  • Invoice Number: Link to an invoice if applicable.
  • Transaction: A brief description of the transaction.
  • Receipt: Optionally upload a receipt in the form of PDF, JPG or PNG only.

Tips

  • Consider adding the method of payment in the Transaction field if you do not have a receipt to upload.
  • Schedule C Tax Categories are automatically assigned based on the Sub-Category you select.
