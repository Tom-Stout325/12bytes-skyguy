Invoice Form Help

Overview

Use this form to create and send professional invoices for services provided, and send a PDF to clients .

Field Descriptions

  • Invoice Number: Enter a unique identifier.
  • Client: Select an existing client, go to the Clients Page to add a new client first.
  • Event: Optional field to reference the event, this will appear on the invoice.
  • Location:Location for the event, this will also appear on the invoice.
  • Service: Choose the service(s) being invoiced.
  • Amount Total amount of the invoice.
  • Date: The invoice issue date.
  • Due Date: When payment is due.
  • Paid Date: Enter the date the invoice is paid, usually done at a later time.
  • Status: Change to Paid when the invoice is paid.
  • Invoice Items: For each line item, add:
    • Description: Brief Description of items being billed
    • Qty: Quantity of the service or item
    • Price: Unit price (automatically calculated)

Tips

  • Use clear descriptions for each invoice item (e.g., “Drone footage – Finals Day”).
  • Link mileage and related expenses after the invoice is created, if applicable.
  • After saving, you can review and export the invoice as a PDF, or email it directly to the client.
